Top Movies of 2025

My Top 5 Movies

A Minecraft Movie:

This family-friendly adventure follows four misfits who are transported into a blocky world, where they must learn to navigate their surroundings and work together to return home.

Thunderbolts:

A unique take on the superhero genre, this film features a team of antiheroes who must confront their pasts while undertaking a dangerous mission

Sinners:

A gripping drama about twin brothers returning to their hometown, only to face a greater evil that awaits them, blending elements of horror and suspense.

From the World of John Wick: Ballerina:

An assassin seeks revenge in this spin-off from the popular John Wick series, promising intense action and a deep dive into the assassin's world.

Mission: Impossible - The Final Reckoning:

Tom Cruise returns as Ethan Hunt in this thrilling conclusion to the iconic franchise, promising high-stakes action and intricate espionage.
